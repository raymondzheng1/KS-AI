NAME: Teachable Machine: Train Your First AI
INTRO:
 - Now you will train a real image classifier — no coding required. You will teach an AI to recognise three hand gestures: thumbs up, peace sign, and fist.
STEPS:
 - Open teachablemachine.withgoogle.com in your browser.
 - Click "Get Started" → "Image Project" → "Standard Image Model."
 - Step 1 — Collect Your Data: Rename "Class 1" to "Thumbs Up." Click "Webcam" and hold your thumb up — record at least 100 images.
 - Rename "Class 2" to "Peace Sign" — record another 100 images.
 - Click "Add a class" → rename to "Fist" — record another 100 images.
 - Step 2 — Train: Click the big "Train Model" button — watch it learn! After training, the "Preview" panel activates automatically.
 - Step 3 — Test: Try each gesture in front of the webcam — does it get them right? Move your hand, change the lighting, use your other hand — does it still work?
 - Try tricking it — show a gesture halfway between two classes. What does it predict?
 - Challenge: If your model makes mistakes, add more training images from different angles and lighting, then retrain. Did adding more data improve accuracy? Record your findings.
